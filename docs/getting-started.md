## Overview
This guide walks you through creating your account and registering your first subdomain.

- Website: `https://isroot.in/`

---

## Step 1 — Visit the Registration Site 🌐
1. Go to `https://isroot.in/`.
2. Click **Get Started** on the landing page.

> Tip: If the page doesn't load, check your network or try a different browser.

![GitHub login screenshot](./images/click%20here%20to%20register.png)

---

## Step 2 — Create an Account 🔒
Currently, the only supported method to create or sign in to an account is **GitHub**.

### Sign in with GitHub (Required)
1. Click **Login** (or **Get Started**) and choose the **GitHub** option.
2. Authenticate with GitHub.
3. If this is your first time signing in, an account will be created automatically using your GitHub email address — make sure your GitHub email is verified.

**Note:** Email/password sign-up is not available at this time. If you need an alternative sign-up method, contact support.



## Step 3 — Navigate to Register Domain ➕
After logging in, click **Register Domain** from the sidebar menu.
![Subdomain creation](./images/click%20here%20to%20register%20domain.png)

## Step 4 — Complete the Registration Form 📝
Follow the steps on the registration form.

### 1) Choose your subdomain name
- Allowed characters: lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens only
- Length: 3–63 characters
- Cannot start or end with a hyphen

Examples:
- ✅ `project` → `project.isroot.in`
- ❌ `ab` (too short)
- ❌ `-myproject` (starts with hyphen)

### 2) Register
Click **Register Domain** to complete registration.
- if domain already exists get creative and find some new name

**What happens next:**
- ✅ Domain is registered immediately
- ✅ DNS delegation automatically configured
- ✅ Domain is valid for 1 year
- ✅ Success message shown in the UI
![Subdomain Page](./images/creating%20the%20subdomain.png)


## Step 5 — Success 
Congratulations — your domain is registered and active!

**Next actions:**
- View your domain in **My Domains**.
- Set up DNS records with your DNS provider (Cloudflare recommended).
- Learn to manage your domain: renewals, nameservers, or deletion.



## Limits & Quotas 
- Default limit: up to **5 domains** per account.
- Domain length: **3–63 characters**.
